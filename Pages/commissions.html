General Information

I do not work with written contracts. However, I do hope that when you commission me, you read my terms of service as they are to be considered rules. This way we can avoid uncomfortable situations or misunderstandings either from me or you. If you have questions, feel free to message me via brendacayzac@gmail.com.

Payments

Payment date will be discussed with the client I accept payments via Paypal.

Deadlines

The finalization dates of each commission depend the complexity of the work. The client will be updated during each step of the work, and there will be rounds of feedback and corrections

Disclaimer

I will not draw : extreme political subjects, gore, vore, big boobs/rear, explicit NSFW, and/or hate images.

When an illustration commission is finished, you will receive a full HD version of it. However, I will not remove the signature and I ask you kindly not to remove it either. No worries, it won't be in the way anything.

My commissions are not created for commercial use or sale. If you plan on making merchandise with it, please let me know.

The clients will receive a full HQ version of their commission and the original editable files if requested. In the case of brand design/identity design, the clients will receive all the .psd, .svg, and or .ai related to the commission.
